# 11 — Conversion

> Turning visitors and signups into paying customers.

Conversion is the art and science of moving people from "interested" to "paying." You've done the hard work of getting them in the door — now you need to prove enough value that they pull out their credit card. This department covers every lever: the funnel, the trial, the pricing, and the checkout experience.

## Why This Matters

A 1% improvement in conversion rate often matters more than a 10% increase in traffic. Conversion optimization is the highest-leverage work in SaaS because it multiplies the value of every upstream dollar you spend on acquisition and distribution.

## How to Work Through This Department

1. **Define your funnel first** — know every stage from awareness to purchase
2. **Nail your pricing** — this is the single strongest lever for revenue
3. **Optimize your trial or freemium model** — whichever acquisition model you use
4. **Streamline checkout** — remove every unnecessary step and friction point
5. **Iterate with data** — use analytics to find and fix conversion bottlenecks

## Key Metrics to Track

- **Visitor-to-signup rate** — website visitors who create an account
- **Trial-to-paid conversion** — free trial users who become paying customers (benchmark: 15-25%)
- **Free-to-paid conversion** — freemium users who upgrade (benchmark: 2-5%)
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)** — tracks pricing effectiveness
- **Time to convert** — average days from signup to first payment
- **Checkout abandonment rate** — users who start checkout but don't finish
