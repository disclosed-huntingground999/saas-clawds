# Proposals and Contracts

## Overview

This folder is for **proposal templates, SOWs, and pricing quotes** — the documents you send after a demo to close the deal. Fast, professional proposals reduce friction and speed up signatures.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What does your proposal include?** (Summary, pricing, terms, timeline?)
2. **Do you use a proposal tool or custom docs?**
3. **What's your pricing quote format?** (Monthly, annual, custom?)
4. **Do you send SOWs for implementation?** (Separate from subscription)
5. **What's your e-signature flow?** (DocuSign, PandaDoc, HelloSign?)
6. **How long are proposals valid?** (30 days? 90?)
7. **Who approves custom pricing?** (Sales, founder, board?)

## Output Template

Create a `README.md` in this folder with:

```markdown
# Proposals and Contracts: [Your SaaS Name]

## Proposal Structure
1. **Executive summary** — Problem, solution, value
2. **Recommended plan** — Tier, features, pricing
3. **Implementation** — Onboarding, timeline
4. **Terms** — Contract length, payment terms
5. **Next steps** — Sign, kickoff

## Proposal Template
| Section | Content | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Cover/Customization | Company name, date, contact | Sales |
| Pricing table | Tiers, add-ons, annual discount | Sales |
| Terms | Link to MSA, SLA | Legal |
| Signature | E-sign block | Sales |

## Pricing Quote Scenarios
| Scenario | Format | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| Standard pricing | List price, no discount | Sales |
| Up to 10% discount | | Sales manager |
| 10–20% discount | | Founder |
| Custom/Enterprise | | Founder + Legal |

## SOW Template (If Applicable)
- **Scope:** Implementation, training, custom work
- **Timeline:** Phases, milestones
- **Deliverables:** 
- **Pricing:** One-time vs recurring
```
