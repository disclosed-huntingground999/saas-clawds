# Sales Hiring

## Overview

This folder is for **hiring your first sales team** — roles, compensation plans, ramp targets, territories, and interview process. Hire when founder-led sales is maxed out and you have a repeatable process to hand off.

## Questions to Answer

1. **When will you hire your first salesperson?** (Pipeline, revenue trigger?)
2. **What role first?** (SDR, AE, full-cycle?)
3. **What's your comp model?** (Base + commission? OTE? Uncapped?)
4. **What's the ramp period?** (Typical: 3–6 months to quota)
5. **How do you split territories?** (By region, vertical, inbound vs outbound?)
6. **What does your interview process look like?** (Ride-along, role-play?)
7. **What tools do they need?** (CRM, enrichment, dialer, etc.)
