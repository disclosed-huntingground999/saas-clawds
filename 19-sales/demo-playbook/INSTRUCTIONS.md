# Demo Playbook

## Overview

This folder is for **demo scripts, objection handling, and competitive positioning** — so every demo tells a compelling story and addresses common concerns. A strong demo increases close rates.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What's your demo flow?** (Discovery first, or product-first?)
2. **How long is your typical demo?** (30 min, 45, 60?)
3. **What's your "aha moment" in the product?** (The one thing that wins them)
4. **What are the top 5 objections you hear?** And your responses
5. **Who are your main competitors?** How do you position against them?
6. **Do you do custom demos or a standard script?**
7. **What follow-up do you send after the demo?**
