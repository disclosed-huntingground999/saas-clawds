# Fundraising

## Overview

This folder is for **raising capital** — pitch deck, financial projections, investor pipeline, cap table, term sheets, and due diligence prep. Only relevant if you're raising; skip if bootstrapped.

## Questions to Answer

1. **Are you raising?** (Pre-seed, seed, Series A, etc.)
2. **How much are you raising?** Target round size
3. **What will you use the funds for?** (18-month plan)
4. **What's your valuation (or target)?** Pre-money
5. **Who are your target investors?** (VCs, angels, strategic)
6. **Do you have a lead investor?** Or still looking
7. **What's your cap table structure?** (Founders, option pool, existing investors)
8. **What documents do investors need?** (Deck, model, data room)
