# 26 — People & Culture: Build the Team and the Environment

> Scaling (16) covers hiring. People & Culture covers **how the team works** — culture, values, performance management, compensation, remote work, and team health. Culture shapes everything.

## Purpose

Hiring is the start; culture determines whether people stay and perform. This department codifies values, performance cycles, compensation philosophy, remote work policy, and team health practices.

| Subfolder | What You'll Produce | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| [culture-values](./culture-values/) | Company values, culture doc, behavioral norms | P0 |
| [performance-management](./performance-management/) | Review cycles, feedback, growth plans | P1 |
| [compensation](./compensation/) | Salary bands, equity, benefits, benchmarking | P1 |
| [remote-work](./remote-work/) | Remote policy, async, tools, rituals | P1 |
| [team-health](./team-health/) | Engagement, 1-on-1s, burnout prevention | P1 |

## How to Work Through This Department

1. **Culture and Values** — Define what you stand for
2. **Performance Management** — Review cycles, feedback, growth
3. **Compensation** — Salary, equity, benefits
4. **Remote Work** — Policy, async, tools
5. **Team Health** — Engagement, 1-on-1s, burnout

## Time Estimate

| Approach | Time |
|---|---|
| Solo/small team (culture + remote) | 1 week |
| With performance + comp (5+ people) | 2–3 weeks |

## Key Principle

**Culture is what you do, not what you say.** Document values and live them. Consistency in hiring, feedback, and decisions builds trust.
