# Compensation

## Overview

This folder is for **compensation philosophy** — salary bands, equity structure, benefits, and benchmarking. Transparent, fair compensation attracts and retains talent.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What's your compensation philosophy?** (Market rate? Top of market? Location-based?)
2. **How do you benchmark salaries?** (Radford? Levels? Pave?)
3. **What's your equity structure?** (Option pool? Vesting? Refresh?)
4. **What benefits do you offer?** (Health, dental, 401k, PTO?)
5. **Do you have salary bands/levels?** (Or market-based?)
6. **How do you handle remote pay?** (Same globally? By location?)
7. **When do you review comp?** (Annual? Promotion? Market shift?)

## Output Template

Create a `README.md` in this folder with:

```markdown
# Compensation: [Your SaaS Name]

## Philosophy
- **Market position:** [50th / 75th / 90th percentile]
- **Location:** [Same for all / By location / By zone]
- **Transparency:** [Bands shared internally / Opaque]
```
