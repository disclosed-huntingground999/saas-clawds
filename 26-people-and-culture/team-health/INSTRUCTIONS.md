# Team Health

## Overview

This folder is for **team health and engagement** — engagement surveys, 1-on-1 structure, burnout prevention, and psychological safety. Healthy teams perform better and stay longer.

## Questions to Answer

1. **Do you run engagement surveys?** (eNPS? Pulse? How often?)
2. **What's your 1-on-1 structure?** (Agenda? Frequency? Who drives?)
3. **How do you prevent burnout?** (PTO enforcement? Workload balance?)
4. **How do you build psychological safety?** (Safe to speak up?)
5. **Do you track burnout signals?** (Overtime, engagement drop?)
6. **What support do you offer?** (EAP? Mental health? Flexibility?)
7. **How do you celebrate wins?** (Shout-outs? Recognition?)
