# Culture and Values

## Overview

This folder is for **company values and culture** — what you stand for, how you behave, and what you prioritize. Values guide hiring, feedback, and decisions as you scale.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What are your 3-5 core values?** (Not buzzwords — specific behaviors)
2. **How did you derive these values?** (Founder beliefs? Early team?)
3. **What does each value look like in practice?** (Concrete examples)
4. **What behaviors violate your values?** (Antipatterns)
5. **How do you use values in hiring?** (Interview questions?)
6. **How do you reinforce values?** (Recognition, reviews?)
7. **Is your culture document public?** (Careers page?)
