# Social Media — Build Presence Where Your Customers Are

## Overview

This folder contains your social media strategy — the plan for building a presence on the platforms where your target customers spend time. Social media is not about being everywhere; it's about being consistently valuable in the 1-2 places that matter most for your audience.

For B2B SaaS, that's usually LinkedIn and Twitter/X. For B2C or creative tools, it might be Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube. The key is choosing the right platforms and showing up with content that educates, entertains, or inspires — not just promotes.

## Questions to Answer

Before generating the social media strategy, the founder needs to answer:

1. **Which 1-2 platforms does your target customer use most actively?** (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, Facebook)
2. **What is your realistic posting frequency?** (Daily, 3-5x/week, weekly — consistency matters more than volume)
3. **What content mix will you use?** (Suggested: 40% educational, 30% engaging/conversational, 20% behind-the-scenes/brand, 10% promotional)
4. **Do you have budget for paid social?** (Even $500/mo of LinkedIn ads can accelerate early traction)
5. **What is your community engagement strategy?** (How much time will you spend replying, commenting on others' posts, and joining conversations?)
6. **Who is the voice of the brand?** (Founder personal brand? Company account? Both?)
7. **What metrics define success?** (Followers? Engagement rate? Click-throughs? Signups attributed to social?)

## Output Template

Generate a comprehensive social media strategy with these sections:

### 1. Platform Strategy
- Primary platform: rationale, audience size, content format
- Secondary platform: rationale, audience size, content format
- Platforms to skip and why (avoid spreading thin)

### 2. Content Calendar
- Weekly posting schedule per platform
- Content themes by day (e.g., Monday = tip, Wednesday = behind-the-scenes, Friday = industry take)
- Mix of original content, repurposed content from blog, and curated/commentary

### 3. Platform-by-Platform Playbook
For each chosen platform:
- Optimal posting times
- Content formats that perform best
- Hashtag / discovery strategy
- Engagement tactics (comment strategy, DM strategy)
- Growth tactics specific to the platform

### 4. Engagement Playbook
- Daily engagement routine (15-30 min/day)
- List of accounts to engage with regularly
- How to respond to comments, DMs, and mentions
- Community participation guidelines

### 5. Paid Social Plan (If Applicable)
- Budget allocation by platform
- Ad formats to test (boosted posts, lead gen, retargeting)
- Audience targeting criteria
- A/B testing framework for creatives

### 6. Metrics & Reporting
- Weekly metrics to track per platform
- Monthly reporting template
- Decision framework: what to double down on vs. drop

## Agent Instructions

When populating this folder, the AI agent should:

1. Read `company-profile.md` to understand the product and target audience
2. Identify the 1-2 best platforms based on whether the audience is B2B (LinkedIn, Twitter) or B2C (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
3. Read `09-acquisition/content-marketing/README.md` to align social content with the broader content strategy
4. Create a weekly content calendar with specific post types per day
5. Write a platform-specific playbook for each chosen platform
6. Include a daily engagement routine with time estimates
7. If the founder has paid social budget, include a basic ad plan
8. Output everything as `README.md` in this folder

## Example Output (Snippet)

```markdown
## Platform Strategy

### Primary: LinkedIn
- **Why:** 78% of B2B decision-makers use LinkedIn. Our ICP (startup CTOs) is highly active here.
- **Posting frequency:** 5x/week (weekdays)
- **Content mix:** Tips (Mon), Industry takes (Tue), Product behind-the-scenes (Wed), Data/insights (Thu), Weekly roundup (Fri)

### Secondary: Twitter/X
- **Why:** Developer and startup community is active. Good for thought leadership and rapid content distribution.
- **Posting frequency:** 2-3x/day (mix of original, reposts, replies)

### Skip: Instagram, TikTok
- **Why:** Our ICP doesn't discover B2B tools on these platforms. Time is better spent elsewhere.

## Weekly Calendar

| Day | LinkedIn | Twitter/X |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Tactical tip (carousel) | Quick tip + link to blog |
| Tue | Hot take on industry trend | Thread on same topic |
| Wed | Behind-the-scenes build update | Engage in 3 relevant threads |
| Thu | Data insight or mini case study | Poll or question |
| Fri | Weekly wins + product update | Repost top-performing LinkedIn post |
```
