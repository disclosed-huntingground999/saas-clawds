# Pricing Intelligence

## Overview

This folder is for **tracking competitor pricing** — list prices, packaging, discounts, and packaging trends. Pricing intel informs your pricing strategy and sales negotiations.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What do competitors charge?** (List prices by tier?)
2. **How do competitors package?** (Seats? Usage? Features?)
3. **Do competitors offer annual discounts?** (How much?)
4. **What's the typical discount in sales?** (If known)
5. **How often do you update pricing intel?** (Quarterly?)
6. **Where do you get pricing info?** (Website, trial, sales?)
7. **How do you use this in sales?** (Battlecards, proposal guidance?)
