# Battlecards

## Overview

This folder is for **sales battlecards** — one-page summaries per competitor with strengths, weaknesses, differentiation, objection handling, and trap-setting questions. Battlecards equip sales to win competitive deals.

## Questions to Answer

1. **Who are the top 3-5 competitors you face in deals?**
2. **What are each competitor's strengths?** (What they say, what wins them deals)
3. **What are each competitor's weaknesses?** (Where they're vulnerable)
4. **How do you differentiate vs each?** (Your angle)
5. **What objections do you hear when competing with X?** (And responses)
6. **What trap questions can you ask?** (Make them admit weakness)
7. **What proof do you have?** (Case studies, benchmarks, reviews)
