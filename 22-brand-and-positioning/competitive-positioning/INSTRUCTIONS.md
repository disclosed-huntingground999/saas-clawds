# Competitive Positioning

## Overview

This folder is for **how you're positioned vs. competitors** — positioning map, differentiation matrix, and strategic positioning choices. Use this for sales, marketing, and product strategy.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What are your 2-3 key differentiators?** (Why choose you?)
2. **How do you position vs. the market leader?** (If applicable)
3. **What's your "category" or "market"?** (How you want to be classified)
4. **What positioning trap do you set for competitors?** (Make them choose between A and B; you offer both)
5. **What do you explicitly NOT do?** (Scope boundaries)
6. **Who are you NOT for?** (Anti-persona)
7. **What's your positioning in one phrase?** (e.g., "The X for Y")
