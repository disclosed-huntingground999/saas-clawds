# Messaging Framework

## Overview

This folder is for **messaging** — value propositions, taglines, positioning statement, elevator pitch, and key messages by audience. Messaging is what you say; it should be consistent across all channels.

## Questions to Answer

1. **What's your one-sentence value proposition?** (For [customer], [product] [benefit] so they can [outcome].)
2. **What's your tagline?** (Short, memorable)
3. **What's your positioning statement?** (For [customer] who [need], [product] is a [category] that [benefit]. Unlike [alternatives], we [differentiator].)
4. **What's your elevator pitch?** (30-60 seconds)
5. **Do you have different messages for different segments?** (SMB vs Enterprise?)
6. **What are your 3-5 key proof points?** (Stats, testimonials, outcomes)
7. **What words do you use?** (And avoid?)

## Output Template

Create a `README.md` in this folder with:

```markdown
# Messaging Framework: [Your SaaS Name]

## Value Proposition (One Sentence)
> For [target customer], [product name] [primary benefit] so they can [outcome] without [pain/constraint].

## Tagline
> [5-10 words max. Memorable. Benefit-focused.]

## Positioning Statement
- **For** [target customer]
- **Who** [need / pain]
```
