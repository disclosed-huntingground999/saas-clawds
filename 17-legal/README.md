# 17 — Legal & Compliance: Protect Your Business

> Every SaaS company runs on contracts, terms, and compliance obligations. This department is where you document and manage the legal foundations that protect you, your customers, and your business — before something goes wrong.

## Purpose

Legal issues are among the most common reasons startups fail or get acquired for less than they could have been worth. A well-structured legal foundation — incorporation, terms, contracts, IP, and compliance — reduces risk and speeds up enterprise deals, fundraising, and international expansion.

This department walks you through five critical areas:

| Subfolder | What You'll Produce | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| [incorporation](./incorporation/) | Entity structure, jurisdiction, formation docs | P0 |
| [terms-and-privacy](./terms-and-privacy/) | ToS, privacy policy, cookie policy, EULA | P0 |
| [contracts](./contracts/) | Customer agreements, vendor contracts, NDAs | P1 |
| [intellectual-property](./intellectual-property/) | Trademarks, patents, trade secrets, open-source | P1 |
| [compliance](./compliance/) | GDPR, CCPA, SOC2, HIPAA frameworks | P1 |

## How to Work Through This Department

1. **Incorporation** — Establish your legal entity
2. **Terms and Privacy** — Protect users and your business with clear legal docs
3. **Contracts** — Standardize agreements with customers and vendors
4. **Intellectual Property** — Protect your brand and innovations
5. **Compliance** — Map regulatory requirements for your market

## Time Estimate

| Approach | Time |
|---|---|
| Bootstrap (DIY + templates + legal counsel review) | 2–4 weeks |
| With dedicated legal counsel from start | 1–2 weeks |

## Key Principle

**Legal is not optional.** "We'll get to it later" leads to acquisition blockers, GDPR fines, and contract disputes. Start with incorporation and terms; layer on contracts and compliance as you scale.
